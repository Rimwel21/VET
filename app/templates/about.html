Our Story

VetSync was founded in 2022 with a simple but powerful mission: to make quality veterinary care more accessible, convenient, and stress-free for both pets and their families.

Frustrated by long phone waits, scattered records, and complicated booking processes, a team of experienced veterinarians and passionate pet parents came together to build a better solution. What started as a small idea to improve appointment booking has grown into a complete pet care platform that connects loving families with trusted veterinary professionals.

Today, VetSync helps thousands of pet owners book appointments 24/7, manage their pet’s health records, discover special offers, and get reliable guidance through our intelligent assistant, ASTRID. We believe technology should bring us closer to better care — not further away from it.

At VetSync, we don’t just treat pets. We care for the special bond between pets and the people who love them.
